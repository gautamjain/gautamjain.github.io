What information do the Applications obtain and how is it used?

The Applications obtain the information you provide when you download and register the Applications. Registration with us is mandatory in order to be able to use the Applications.

When you register with us and use the Applications, you generally provide (a) your name, email address, phone, and other registration information; (b) transaction-related information, such as when you make purchases, respond to any offers, or download or use applications from us; (c) information you provide us when you contact us for help, and; (d) credit card information for purchase and use of the Applications.

We may also use the information you provided us to contact your from time to time to provide you with important information, required notices and marketing promotions.

Automatically Collected Information

In addition, the Applications may collect certain information automatically, including, but not limited to, the type of mobile device you use, your mobile device's unique device ID, the IP address of your mobile device, your mobile operating system, the type of mobile Internet browsers you use, the approximate location at which the Applications are being used, and information about the way you use the Applications.

Do the Applications collect information about my contacts?

The Applications do not collect any information about the contacts on your mobile device - including contact names, phones, emails, and other contact details.

Do the Applications collect real time location information of the device?

When you use the Applications, we may use GPS technology (or other similar technology) to determine your current approximate location in order to determine the city or region you are located within. We will not share your location with other users or partners. We may share your location with our advertising and analytics partners. Approximate location may be derived from network location sources such as cell towers and Wi-Fi.

Only aggregated, anonymized data is periodically transmitted to external services to help us improve the Applications and our service. We will share your information with third parties only in the ways that are described in this privacy statement.

You can stop all collection of information by the Applications easily by uninstalling the Applications. You may use the standard uninstall processes as may be available as part of your mobile device or via the mobile application marketplace or network.
